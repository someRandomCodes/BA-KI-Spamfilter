              <br /> The tradition of tapas is rooted in social engagement and camaraderie. Each small dish is designed to be shared, fostering a sense of community and conversation. Tapas bars in Spain are bustling, vibrant places where friends and family gather to enjoy delicious food and engage in lively conversation. 
              <br /> 
              <br /> Theres a rich variety to the world of tapas. Cold tapas might include dishes like albondigas, delicious meatballs in sauce, or boquerones, white anchovies marinated in vinegar. Hot tapas can vary widely, including dishes like gambas al ajillo, juicy shrimp sauted with garlic, or patatas bravas, fried potatoes topped with a spicy tomato sauce.
