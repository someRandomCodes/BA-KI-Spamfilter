The concept of 'do-it-yourself' began to gain popularity as homeowners took on more home improvement projects on their own.

The mid-20th century saw a significant shift in the retail landscape.  With the rise of shopping malls and big-box retailers, many small hardware stores faced challenges.  However, they adapted by joining cooperatives to improve their buying power and competitive position.
