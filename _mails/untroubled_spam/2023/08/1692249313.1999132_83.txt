Home improvement stores became the go-to places for materials, tools, and advice. 

In today's digital age, many of these stores have adopted online platforms, making it even easier for customers to browse and purchase products.  From virtual workshops to online consultations, the digital transformation has enabled these establishments to reach a broader audience and offer more personalized services.
