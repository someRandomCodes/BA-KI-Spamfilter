Home improvement and maintenance items, like tools, automotive products, and garden supplies, are also readily available.  The selection ranges from plants and soil to power tools and car care products.

Furniture is another category where Costco shines.  From living room sets to bedroom furniture and office desks, the range and quality are unmatched, especially when considering the value for the price.

Automotive lovers will appreciate the tire centers and car buying services.  Whether it's getting a new set of tires, having them rotated, or even buying a new car, Costco has got it covered. 

The holiday season sees an influx of seasonal items.  From Halloween costumes to Christmas decor, the store transforms to cater to every festive need. 

Lastly, the alcohol section in clubs (where permitted) offers a range of wines, beers, and spirits.  Whether it's a fine wine or a bulk pack of beer for a party, members can find it at Costco. 

In conclusion, Costco's vast product range ensures that members can find almost everything they need under one roof, and all at unbeatable prices.
