Earn Top Credentials. Get Certified in Electrical Safety Management and Oversight.

Earning your NFPA® Certified Electrical Safety Compliance Professional (CESCP) credential can help take your career to the next level by demonstrating your expertise and commitment to helping keep others safe.

NFPA CESCP certification is based on the industry-leading standards we develop at NFPA, so you can be assured that it is the most thorough way to prove your experience, expertise, skills, and knowledge in electrical safety. CESCP can also be invaluable for trainers and instructors, as it provides credibility and demonstrates competence in NFPA 70E®, Standard for Electrical Safety in the Workplace®.

Having a proven record of safety certification means success at every level.

The CESCP program:
*     Provides recognition and evidence of competence as an electrical safety compliance professional related to NFPA 70E
*     Improves marketability and serves as a differentiator between certification holders and their peers
*     Increases earning power and supports career advancement for certification holders
*     Promotes professional development through continuous learning
*     Promotes electrical safety in workplace environments to reduce costs and help keep people safe
*     Ensures a uniform, fair process for certification that is accessible to everyone who is eligible

Take hold of your career and business. Get started on your CESCP certification today.
