NFPA® safety certification means success at every level. Currently based on the 20th edition of the NFPA Fire Protection Handbook®, the Certified Fire Protection Specialist (CFPS®) certification reflects your mastery of key skills and knowledge. A CFPS certified professional has a proven command of fire protection and prevention standards and is recognized for having the skills and expertise necessary to curtail fire loss, both physical and financial.

Over 5,000 people have earned a CFPS by demonstrating their leadership through applied work experience, related education opportunities, and successful completion of a certification examination. These professionals include risk managers, loss control specialists, fire officers, fire marshals, fire inspectors, safety managers, fire protection consultants, designers, engineers, code enforcers, facility managers, and others who have responsibilities dealing with the application of fire safety, protection, prevention, and suppression technologies.

In January 2024, the CFPS exam will be updated to reflect material in the new 21st edition of the Fire Protection Handbook (https://go.nfpa.org/e/14662/tm-content-cert-order-src-f098/8kpfrn/1911546217?h=knMShGNcrKYxqj1yHOA_fjQecEwcuDcOMN-T1CW2nJA).
